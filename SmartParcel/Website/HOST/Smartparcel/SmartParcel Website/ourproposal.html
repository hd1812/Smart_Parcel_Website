Smart Parcel Receiver

 

Abstract----Almost a billion parcels are estimated to have been delivered in the UK in 2012[1], but 12% of deliveries fail first time, costing the industry approximately £1bn in re-deliveries [2]. As second year students, we experienced this since we moved to self-rented house, which normally has no reception. In order to solve this problem, we come up with the idea of creating a smart parcel receiver system, which aims to improve the quality of parcel delivery service.

What needs to be solved?

This project provides a different solution for current mailing system, and it will reduce the number of deliveries which fail first time. So that experience of customer  will be better and the cost for the courier company will decrease. The project is to construct a smart system, which promotes communication between postmen and householders. It will also be able to find the nearby householders who are willing to substitute for collecting parcel.

Our final product consists of both hardware and software. Hardware part is a ‘smart messenger’, which is a smal device hanged on door with multiple functions. It is mainly composed of a screen, a keyboard, a mini-camera and an audio recorder. While software is a mobile application and a official service website.

This system is designed to avoid situations which cause delivery failure. Although there are many factors that cause delivery failure, we will mainly focus on one of them---householder is not at home. Working procedure is explained as the following. Before parcel arriving, householder can leave information about the intended parcel on ‘smart messenger’ through our website or mobile application, such as item name, reference, description, estimated arrival date, preferred dropping place etc. Postman verifies delivery address and leave item at the preferred place.

If the householder didn’t know the parcel delivery date and didn’t leave a message, the system will allow postman to take and send a photo of the information about the parcel to the householder through the smart messenger. Householder will get information through the mobile application and can send message back. Householder can choose to send the address of the prefer dropping place, or use our client network to find someone nearby who has also registered with our system and is happy to substitute to collect the parcel. Consequently, cost of redelivery is saved. Householder will get the parcel quickly as well. And if the parcel is dropped at someone else’s, the application will generate and send a security code for both householder and the one collected the parcel. They will be able to match their code through the mobile application to collect the parcel.

So this system will not only make the communication between
the postman and the householder, but also a group of registered clients, who live nearby and are willing to help. Under the condition of safety and privacy, they might help each other to collect parcels.

What research needs to be done?

In general, we need to search for courier companies and negotiate with them to cooperate. We need to know their general delivery rules and the problems they have met. We will also need to learn the relative laws and the right of privacy of the householders who are willing to register with this system.

With respect to the software design part, we will need to do research on the area of IOS and android application design. Knowledge about user interface design, website building as well as the database construction study is also desirable. In addition, the research on information exchange technology between the mobile app, website and the smart messenger at the door is significant.

For the hardware construction part, research on the components assembly is in our plan, since hardware device body is composed of different components such as keyboard, screen, camera, recorder and internet connection. In addition, surface design is necessary in making its appearance appealing.

What expertise is needed?

This proposal covers the area of signal communication (the information exchange between the mobile app and the smart messenger), analogue and digital electronics (the data process in the smart messenger), and programming (building website, database and the mobile application).

The design of the system covers a wide range in engineering, so we wish that the team members will have a specialized interest in each of them. 

The team members are encouraged to not only specialized in one area, but also get to know the others. They are also required to have good skills to communicate within group, as well as with supervisors. This project is highly open-minded, so we wish all the team members are creative. Every member should have a good time keeping and enthusiasm on engineering problems.
